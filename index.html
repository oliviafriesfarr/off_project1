Olivia Fries-Farr ~ Mid - Residence

I sang and played rhythm guitar in this showcase comprised mostly of original music I have been writing and working on over the last year and a half. This was my first performance in over two years! Check out the livestream if you missed it in person!

Featuring:

  • Andrew Bedows (piano)
  • Caroline Baughman (drums)
  • Sam Brancaccio (lead guitar)
  • Kira Markman (violin)
  • Michaella Moon (cello)
  • Callen O'Brien (bass)
